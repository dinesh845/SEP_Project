Lesson 1: Understanding Sustainability

Sustainability means meeting our needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs. It’s about balancing environmental, social, and economic priorities to keep the planet healthy.

  • Reduce waste and pollution
  • Use renewable resources
  • Protect natural ecosystems

Lesson 2: Recycling & Waste Management

Every item you throw away has an impact. Learn how to reduce, reuse, and recycle to make a positive difference!

  • Use reusable bottles and bags
  • Sort your trash properly
  • Compost organic waste

Lesson 3: Energy Conservation

Saving energy helps reduce carbon emissions. Small changes, like turning off lights or using public transport, make a big impact.
